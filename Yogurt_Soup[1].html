Yogurt Soup

This is a recipe for yogurt soup.

Turkish measurements have been used!

Ingredients

  • Olive or any other liquid oil,
  • 2 tablespoons of heaping flour,
  • warm water,
  • 1 bowl of whisked yogurt,
  • 3 teaspoons of sea salt,
  • 2 teaspoons of dried mint,
  • 4 tablespoons of washed cracked wheat or rice.

Instructions

  1. Pour enough olive oil to cover the bottom of the pot, then heat it up.
  2. After a while, add a pinch of flour to see if it's dancing in the oil. Then add the rest of the flour.
  3. Whisk the flour inside the oil with a whisk.
  4. Pour warm water in the pot to somehwat fill it to the top (but not fully).
  5. Immediately add the yogurt and whisk continuously.
  6. Add salt and mint and continue whisking.
  7. Add preffered amount of wheat or rice and whisk for 2-3 more minutes, so it doesn't stick to the pan and burn.
  8. Once it boils, lower the heat and leave the pot's lid slightly open.
  9. Let it boil 10 more minutes in low heat.
  10. If it boils longer, it will get thicker. However you prefer <3
